Dialecticke otherwise called Logicke, is an arte which teachethe to dispute well.Dialectic, otherwise called Logic, is an art which teaches to dispute well.
It is diuyded into two partes: Inuention, and iudgement or disposition.It is divided into two parts: Invention and judgment or disposition.
Inuention is the first parte of Dialecticke, whiche teachethe to inuente argumentes.Invention is the first part of Dialectic, which teaches to invent arguments.
